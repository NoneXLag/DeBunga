African Lily

African-lily is generally regarded as an easy-to-care-for perennial. Key care points include ensuring adequate sunlight exposure and protection from intense afternoon sun. Additionally, african-lily requires regular watering during the growing season but less during the dormant period. Not susceptible to many pests, african-lily thrives with minimal intervention, making it ideal for gardeners seeking low-maintenance plants.

Property Value
Scientific Name Agapanthus africanus
Family Amaryllidaceae
Native Region South Africa
